Evaluation Framework

LITMUS

How GhostLedger evaluates claims before coordination ever begins. Six principles. No opinions. Structured signal.

What LITMUS is

LITMUS is GhostLedger's internal framework for determining whether a claim is real, actionable, and worth coordinating around.

It is not a legal opinion. It is not a judgment. It is a signal of seriousness.

Every claim that enters GhostLedger is evaluated against six principles before any coordination, escalation, or professional referral occurs. Claims that do not meet the threshold do not advance. Claims that pass are treated as legitimate recovery candidates.

The six principles

L
Legitimate Obligation

Is there a real, owed obligation? LITMUS begins by asking whether money was actually earned, promised, or contractually due. If the claim is speculative, aspirational, or based on misunderstanding, it does not advance.

I
Independent Verifiability

Can the claim stand on its own, without hype, narrative, or speculation? Real financial obligations exist regardless of market conditions, public sentiment, or platform status. If the claim only makes sense in a specific context, it is fragile.

T
Tactical Readiness

Has the claimant already tried reasonable steps? LITMUS favors claims where the claimant has made good-faith attempts — contacted support, documented the issue, followed the platform's process. Coordination is not a first resort; it is a force multiplier.

M
Material Evidence

Is there more than promises or screenshots? Contracts, invoices, payout records, correspondence, bank statements — material evidence separates recoverable claims from noise. Promises or informal assurances alone do not meet the threshold.

U
Unresolved Harm

Is the harm ongoing or meaningfully unresolved? If the platform already paid, if the dispute was settled, if the claimant received compensation — there is nothing to coordinate. LITMUS only advances active, open obligations.

S
Systemic Signal

Does this appear isolated — or repeated? When multiple claimants report the same respondent, the same pattern, the same withholding behavior, the signal strengthens. Systemic patterns receive elevated attention and coordination priority through aggregation.

Why this exists

Coordination without standards becomes noise. Every platform, every forum, every group chat is full of complaints. Most go nowhere because no one separates signal from noise.

LITMUS exists so GhostLedger only escalates claims that deserve attention — and filters those that don't. It protects claimants from wasted effort, protects professionals from unqualified referrals, and protects the system from becoming another complaint box.

Public Disclosure

LITMUS scoring operates internally inside GhostLedger. The principles described on this page explain how GhostLedger thinks — not how it calculates. Internal scoring logic, thresholds, and weighting are not publicly disclosed.

No LITMUS evaluation constitutes legal advice, legal judgment, or determination of liability. GhostLedger does not provide legal advice or legal representation. LITMUS does not replace independent professional judgment.
